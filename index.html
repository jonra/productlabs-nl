Frequently asked questions

Common questions about Claude AI solutions

Everything you need to know about working with Anthropic's Claude through ProductLabs.

What is Anthropic's Claude and why should my business care?
Claude is Anthropic's frontier AI assistant — one of the most capable large language models available. It excels at reasoning, analysis, coding, and natural language tasks. For businesses, Claude can automate knowledge work, power intelligent product features, and handle complex workflows that previously required human judgment. ProductLabs helps you identify where Claude creates the most value and builds the integrations to make it happen.
What is the Model Context Protocol (MCP)?
MCP is an open standard developed by Anthropic that lets Claude connect to external tools, databases, and services. Think of MCP servers as bridges between Claude and your business systems — they allow Claude to read your data, take actions in your tools, and automate end-to-end workflows. ProductLabs builds custom MCP servers tailored to your specific tech stack and business needs.
What are Claude skills and plugins?
Claude skills are specialized instruction packages that give Claude domain-specific capabilities — like generating legal documents, analyzing financial data, or following your brand guidelines. Plugins bundle multiple skills, MCP servers, and tools into installable packages. ProductLabs designs and builds custom skills and plugins that make Claude an expert in your specific workflows, ready to deploy across your team.
Do I need a large team or budget to use Claude?
Not at all. Anthropic's Claude API is accessible and pay-per-use, which makes it ideal for startups and SMBs. You don't need an in-house ML team or massive infrastructure. ProductLabs handles the technical implementation — from API integration to prompt engineering to deployment — so you get enterprise-grade AI capabilities at a fraction of the cost of building in-house.
How long does a typical Claude integration take?
Most Claude integrations go from scoping to production in 2-6 weeks, depending on complexity. A simple API integration or MCP server might take 1-2 weeks. A full agentic workflow with multiple tool integrations typically takes 4-6 weeks. We scope every project upfront so there are no surprises.
